The brick it's where it's going show/display everything!

The words/numbers /pictures will be in pixels.

"Pixels" are like tiny, colorful building blocks that make up pictures on your computer, phone, or TV screen.

* Notice that you can make the brick 3 different light color (green, red, and orange)
